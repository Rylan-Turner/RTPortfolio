About Me

The "Scene It Before" project I had to do as part of my final Senior Project class bolstered my capabilites to document things professionally and work effectively with a team of 6 people. I hope that this portfolio incentivizes you to contact me so that I can become a part of your team and create an impact on the world of technology.
